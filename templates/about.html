Our history



Our passion for Italian cuisine has been a long-lasting love affair. Back in 1990, two wise men named Mario and Luigi noticed that pizza in Italy could be improved. That's when they decided to start a business, crafting authentic Italian pizza and other delicacies. And so, the first-ever "Mario and Luigi's Pizzeria" was born, serving the best pizza that Italy deserved.

Today, Mario and Luigi still lead the company, adhering to their own principles. With the help of Fontys' students (group 1-2), they continue to develop their vision of perfect pizza.


What do we do?

At "Mario and Luigi's Pizzeria," we create an unforgettable dining experience. We bring your table to life with fantastic food, music, and company. Our goal is to make you smile, lift your spirits, and maybe even get some sauce on your shirt!

In our from-scratch kitchen, we carefully combine fresh, locally sourced, and organic ingredients into delicious dishes. From our house-made mozzarella to our fresh daily dough and tomato sauces, we prepare each meal with the same love and attention you would find in your own home. That's what makes our food more than just great – it's an experience!

When you dine with us, it's like gathering with friends, neighbors, and family. We want to provide our community with a place to eat, drink, and unwind. Pursuing the perfect pizza is our tribute to all those who have made pizza what it is today. Our menu is steeped in age-old traditions and cooking methods, using only the best quality ingredients. With our unique ambiance, daily made-from-scratch offerings, and exceptional guest service, we strive to make your dining experience with us truly amazing.
